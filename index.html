About us

Here at Volume, we’re passionate about bringing great climbing to you and bringing great climbers together. Our brand new, state of the art centre in the center of Birmingham boasts hundreds of routes for both bouldering and rope climbing set by world class route setters. Our friendly, knowledgeable staff are always eager to help and our expert training courses will see you beating your climbing goals no matter your current skill level. Whether you're interested in learning something new, training hard, challenging yourself or just meeting new, exciting people, join us at Volume.

Volumes are AWESOME! In Case you’re new to the term, a volume in climbing is an extremely large type of hold. They come in all sorts of crazy shapes and are so big you can attach other holds to them. They often change the shape of sections of wall and so can create near infinite variety in route setting and climbing styles. Our founder, Markus Bowden, LOVES volumes, and when it came time to name our new centre it felt like a no brainer. That’s what we are. We change the shape of the wall and hope to bring people together by promoting variety in all things climbing. We ask people to think and work together to figure out the solution. And more than anything, we’re a whole lot of fun! We hope to see you all on the walls having fun really soon.

Coffee and Climb

Looking to meet up with other climbers? Need help with a problem? Love great coffee? Our groups meet every week to chat and catch up over a hot drink before heading to the walls. Group climbing is a great way to learn from others and colaborate on beta, building your skills and teamwork while you strive to climb harder problems. Coffee and Climb is open to all, regardless of skill and experience. So whether you're looking for a belay partner or an extra set of eyes on your latest tricky climbing problem, come along and join in.

Climbing Taster Sessions

New to climbing? Our taster sessions are just what you need to get started! You’ll be paired up with one of our expert instructors who will teach you everything you need to have fun and stay safe on the walls. From hold types to heel hooks and more; dive into your new hobby with knowledge and confidence. This sessions also includes climbing shoe and harness hire so all you need to bring along is yourself and a desire to learn.
