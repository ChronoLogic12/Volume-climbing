Coffee and Climb

Looking to meet up with other climbers? Need help with a problem? Love great coffe? Our groups meet every week to chat and catch up over a hot drink before heading to the walls. Group climbing is a great way to learn from others and colaborate on beta, building your skills and teamwork while you strive to climb harder problems. Cofee and Climb is open to all, regardless of skill and experience. So whether you're looking for a belay partner or an extra set of eyes on your latest tricky climbing problem, come along and join in.

Every wednesday and saturday 10:00 - 12:30

Climbing Taster Sessions

New to climbing? Our taster sessions are just what you need to get started! You’ll be pared up with one of our expert instructors who will teach you everything you need to have fun and stay safe on the walls. From hold types to heel hooks and more; dive into your new hobby with knowledge and confidence. This sessions also includes climbing shoe and harness hire so all you need to bring along is yourself and a desire to learn.

Technique Masterclass

No matter your skill level or how long you’ve been climbing there’s always more to learn. Our teams incredible instructors and route setters will be putting on a masterclass to help you improove your climbing technique and top those difficult problems like a pro!
